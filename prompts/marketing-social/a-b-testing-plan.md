# A/B Testing Plan

**Category:** Marketing & Social  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Create a plan to test and optimize different aspects of your digital presence to improve conversion rates.
> Structure:
> - Hypothesis: What we believe will happen and why
> - Variable being tested: (headline / CTA / layout / image / copy)
> - Control version: [current]
> - Test version: [proposed change]
> - Success metric: (CVR / CTR / time on page / bounce rate)
> - Minimum sample size needed and estimated duration
> - How to declare a winner (statistical significance threshold: 95%)
> Run one test at a time. Document results before moving to the next.
