# Optimize Blog Post for SEO

**Category:** Marketing & Social  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Suggest 5 new SEO keywords for a blog post and show how to include them.
> For each keyword:
> 1. State where to insert it (title, H2, body paragraph, meta description, alt text)
> 2. Show the original sentence and the rewritten version with the keyword naturally included
> 3. Confirm the keyword intent matches the article topic
> Do not keyword-stuff. Readability takes priority over density.
