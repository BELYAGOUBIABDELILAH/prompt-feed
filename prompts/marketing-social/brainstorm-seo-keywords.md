# Brainstorm SEO Keywords

**Category:** Marketing & Social  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Brainstorm and create SEO keywords for marketing website content.
> Output format:
> - Primary keyword (highest volume, most relevant): [x]
> - 5 secondary keywords (supporting terms): [list]
> - 5 long-tail keywords (specific, lower competition): [list]
> - 3 competitor gap keywords (terms competitors rank for that we don't): [list]
> For each keyword, include: estimated intent (informational / commercial / transactional) and difficulty (Low / Medium / High).
