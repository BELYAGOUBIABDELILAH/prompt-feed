# Create Social Media Copy for Employees

**Category:** Marketing & Social  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Create social media posts for employees to use for key marketing and product launches and campaigns.
> Format:
> - LinkedIn version (professional tone, 150–200 words)
> - Twitter/X version (punchy, under 280 characters)
> - Include 3–5 relevant hashtags per version
> - Provide a suggested image description for the post
> Keep the voice authentic — employees sharing, not corporate broadcasting.
