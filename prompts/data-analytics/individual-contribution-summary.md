# Individual Contribution Summary

**Category:** Data & Analytics  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> Generate a comprehensive contribution summary for: [USERNAME]
> Time period: [DATE RANGE]
> Scope: [PROJECT/GROUP]
> 
> Include the following metrics:
> 
> ## Merge Requests Authored
> - Total MRs opened
> - Total MRs merged
> - Average time from open to merge
> - Largest MRs by lines changed
> 
> ## Code Review Activity
> - Total MRs reviewed
> - Average review turnaround time
> - Review comments left
> 
> ## Issue and Work Item Activity
> - Issues created
> - Issues closed
> - Issues assigned and completed
> 
> ## Summary Narrative
> Write a 3–4 sentence professional summary suitable for a performance review, capturing the quality and impact of the contributions — not just the volume.
> 
> Note: Focus on signal over noise. A developer who closes 5 complex issues matters more than 20 trivial ones.
