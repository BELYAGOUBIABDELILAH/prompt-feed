# Open Issues by Priority and Health Status

**Category:** Data & Analytics  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> Provide a breakdown of all currently open issues in: [PROJECT/GROUP]
> 
> ## By Priority
> Group issues into: Critical / High / Medium / Low / No Priority
> Show count and percentage for each tier.
> 
> ## By Health Status
> Group issues by their health label: On Track / Needs Attention / At Risk / No Status
> Show count for each.
> 
> ## Priority × Health Matrix
> Create a 2D view showing which priority issues are most at risk.
> 
> ## Top 5 Issues Needing Immediate Attention
> List the 5 highest-priority issues that are either "At Risk" or have had no activity in 7+ days.
> Format: Issue title · Priority · Days since last activity · Assignee
> 
> ## Recommendation
> In 2–3 sentences, summarize where the team should focus this sprint.
