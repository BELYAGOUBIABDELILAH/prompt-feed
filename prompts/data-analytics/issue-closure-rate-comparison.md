# Issue Closure Rate Comparison

**Category:** Data & Analytics  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> Analyze issue closure rates across the past [N] months for: [PROJECT/GROUP]
> 
> For each month, provide:
> - Total issues opened
> - Total issues closed
> - Net change (opened - closed)
> - Closure rate percentage (closed / opened × 100)
> 
> Then provide:
> 
> ## Trend Analysis
> - Is the team accelerating, decelerating, or stable?
> - Identify any months with notable spikes or drops
> - Highlight any correlation with sprint cadence, releases, or team size changes
> 
> ## Health Signal
> Rate the team's throughput health as:  Healthy /  Watch /  Concern
> Explain the rating in 2 sentences.
> 
> Output as a table + short narrative. Keep it scannable.
