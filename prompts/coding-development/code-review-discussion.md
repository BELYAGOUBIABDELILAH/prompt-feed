# Code Review Discussion

**Category:** Coding & Development  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> Help me understand the design decisions in this merge request:
> 
> MR: [LINK or PASTE DIFF]
> 
> I want to understand:
> 
> ## What Changed and Why
> 1. What problem does this MR solve?
> 2. Why was this approach chosen over alternatives?
> 3. What assumptions does the implementation make?
> 
> ## Architecture Decisions
> - What design patterns were applied?
> - How does this fit into the existing architecture?
> - What contracts/interfaces does this change?
> 
> ## Tradeoffs Made
> For each significant decision in this MR:
> - What was gained? (performance, readability, simplicity)
> - What was sacrificed? (flexibility, complexity, technical debt)
> - Was this the right tradeoff for our context?
> 
> ## Questions to Ask the Author
> Generate 3–5 thoughtful review questions that:
> - Challenge assumptions without being confrontational
> - Explore edge cases the author may not have considered
> - Invite explanation of non-obvious choices
> 
> ## What I'd Do Differently (if anything)
> Be specific. If the current approach is fine, say so.
