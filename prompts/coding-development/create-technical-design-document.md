# Create Technical Design Document

**Category:** Coding & Development  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> Create a comprehensive technical design document (TDD/RFC):
> 
> Feature/System: [NAME]
> Author: [YOUR NAME]
> Date: [DATE]
> Status: [Draft / In Review / Approved]
> 
> ## Technical Design Document
> 
> ### Summary
> [2–3 sentences: what are we building and why?]
> 
> ### Background and Motivation
> - Current situation: [what exists today]
> - Problem: [what's broken or missing]
> - Why now: [business driver or urgency]
> 
> ### Goals
> ✅ In scope:
> - [Goal 1]
> - [Goal 2]
> 
> ❌ Out of scope:
> - [Explicit exclusion 1 — prevents scope creep]
> 
> ### Design
> 
> #### High-Level Architecture
> [Text description of the major components and how they interact]
> [Include an ASCII diagram or describe what a diagram would show]
> 
> #### Key Design Decisions
> For each significant decision, use this format:
> 
> **Decision**: [What we decided]
> **Options considered**:
>   - Option A: [pros/cons]
>   - Option B: [pros/cons]
>   - Option C: [pros/cons]
> **Chosen**: Option [X]
> **Rationale**: [Why, tied to goals and constraints]
> 
> #### Data Model
> [Key entities and their relationships]
> 
> #### API Design
> [Key endpoints or interfaces]
> 
> #### Error Handling
> [How failures are handled and surfaced]
> 
> ### Success Metrics
> How will we measure success?
> - [Metric 1]: target [value]
> - [Metric 2]: target [value]
> 
> ### Risks and Open Questions
> | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
> |---|---|---|---|
