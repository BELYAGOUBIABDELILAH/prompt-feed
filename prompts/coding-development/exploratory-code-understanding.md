# Exploratory Code Understanding

**Category:** Coding & Development  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> I'm exploring an unfamiliar codebase and need your help understanding it. Let's do this conversationally.
> 
> Repository/scope: [DESCRIBE]
> 
> Start by helping me answer these questions:
> 
> ## Map of the Territory
> 1. What is the entry point of this application?
> 2. What are the major modules/packages and what does each own?
> 3. What does the folder structure tell us about the architecture?
> 4. What are the 5 most important files to understand first?
> 
> ## Dependency Graph
> - What are the core external dependencies and why are they used?
> - What is the internal dependency order (which modules depend on which)?
> - Are there any circular dependencies?
> 
> ## Data Flow
> - Where does data enter the system?
> - How does it flow through the layers?
> - Where does it exit (database, API response, file, event)?
> 
> ## Ask Me What You Don't Know
> After providing the above, ask me 3 clarifying questions that would help you give me better explanations of this codebase.
