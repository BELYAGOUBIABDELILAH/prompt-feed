# Rubber Duck Debugging with AI

**Category:** Coding & Development  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> I'm going to explain my bug to you. Help me debug it by asking structured follow-up questions and reflecting my reasoning back to me.
> 
> ## Problem Description
> [DESCRIBE YOUR BUG — what's happening vs. what you expect]
> 
> ## Rubber Duck Protocol
> After I describe the bug, do the following:
> 
> 1. **Reflect back**: Summarize what I told you in your own words to confirm you understood correctly.
> 
> 2. **Clarifying Questions** (ask these one at a time):
>    - When did this last work correctly?
>    - What changed recently (code, config, data, environment)?
>    - What does the error message say exactly?
>    - What have I already tried?
> 
> 3. **Hypothesis Generation**: Based on my answers, generate 3 hypotheses ranked by likelihood:
>    - Hypothesis 1 (most likely): [...]
>    - How to test it: [specific step]
>    - Hypothesis 2: [...]
>    - How to test it: [specific step]
>    - Hypothesis 3: [...]
>    - How to test it: [specific step]
> 
> 4. **Next Action**: Give me one specific, concrete thing to try first.
> 
> 5. **If Still Stuck**: Help me write a minimal reproduction case.
> 
> Start by asking me to describe the problem.
