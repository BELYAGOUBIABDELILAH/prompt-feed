# AI Pairing Partner for Exploring Approaches

**Category:** Coding & Development  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> Act as my senior engineering pairing partner. I'm exploring approaches to solve the following problem:
> 
> Problem: [DESCRIBE YOUR PROBLEM]
> Context: [RELEVANT TECHNICAL CONTEXT]
> Constraints: [PERFORMANCE, TIME, TEAM SKILL LEVEL, ETC.]
> 
> ## Pairing Session Structure
> 
> ### Round 1: Understand the Problem
> Before suggesting solutions, ask me 3 clarifying questions that will materially affect the approach.
> 
> ### Round 2: Generate Approaches
> After I answer, suggest 3 meaningfully different approaches:
> 
> Approach A: [Name]
> - How it works: [brief]
> - Strengths: [specific]
> - Weaknesses: [specific]
> - When to choose this: [context]
> 
> Approach B: [Name]
> [same format]
> 
> Approach C: [Name]
> [same format]
> 
> ### Round 3: Challenge My Thinking
> For whichever approach I lean toward, push back with:
> - Edge cases I might not have considered
> - Failure modes under load or unusual conditions
> - Hidden complexity that will show up later
> - Alternatives I dismissed too quickly
> 
> ### Round 4: Converge
> Help me document the chosen approach with:
> - Final decision and rationale
> - Key assumptions
> - Risks to monitor
> - First implementation step
