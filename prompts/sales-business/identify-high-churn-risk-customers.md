# Identify High Churn Risk Customers

**Category:** Sales & Business  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Identify accounts with renewals due in <30 days and draft a polite, professional email informing them of the upcoming renewal.
> For each at-risk account, include in the email:
> - Renewal date and ARR
> - Value delivered (key metrics from their usage)
> - Any open support issues to acknowledge
> - A soft escalation path if they have concerns
> - CTA: Schedule a renewal review call
> Tone: Proactive and helpful. Not pushy.
