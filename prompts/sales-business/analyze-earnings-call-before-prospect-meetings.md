# Analyze Earnings Call Before Prospect Meetings

**Category:** Sales & Business  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Analyze earnings call for prospect outreach.
> From the earnings call transcript or summary, extract:
> 1. Top 3 strategic priorities mentioned by leadership
> 2. Key challenges or headwinds they acknowledged
> 3. Any technology investments or initiatives relevant to our product
> 4. Quotes that could be used as conversation openers
> 5. Recommended talking points that connect our value prop to their stated priorities
> Output: A 1-page prospect prep brief.
