# Draft Personalized Outreach Post-Sale

**Category:** Sales & Business  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Create a personalized email outreach sequence with a customer after the sale.
> Sequence structure:
> Email 1 (Day 1): Thank you + quick-start resource
> Email 2 (Day 7): Check-in + specific tip based on their use case
> Email 3 (Day 21): Case study relevant to their industry + upsell seed
> Email 4 (Day 45): Health check + renewal/expansion conversation invite
> Each email: Under 100 words. One CTA. Personalize with [CUSTOMER NAME], [USE CASE], [COMPANY].
