# Objection Handling

**Category:** Sales & Business  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Create a personalized response to a customer pain point based on their industry and role.
> For the objection: [OBJECTION]
> 1. Acknowledge the concern genuinely (1 sentence)
> 2. Reframe it with relevant context (1–2 sentences)
> 3. Address it with evidence: data point, case study, or feature explanation
> 4. Provide a next step that reduces risk for them (trial, pilot, demo, reference call)
> Keep under 150 words. Avoid sounding defensive or salesy.
