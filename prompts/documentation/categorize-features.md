> Categorize the following feature list using MoSCoW prioritization:
> 
> Features to categorize:
> [PASTE YOUR FEATURE LIST]
> 
> Context for prioritization:
> - Product vision: [DESCRIBE]
> - Target users: [DESCRIBE]
> - Release deadline: [DATE]
> - Team capacity: [N developer-weeks]
> - Business constraint: [REVENUE / COMPLIANCE / COMPETITIVE / etc.]
> 
> ## MoSCoW Categorization
> 
> ###  MUST HAVE
> Non-negotiable for launch. Without these, the product fails.
> [List features with 1-line justification each]
>

> [List features with 1-line justification each]
>

> [List features with 1-line justification each]
> 
> ### ⚪ WON'T HAVE (this release)
> Explicitly out of scope. Document for future roadmap.
> [List features with 1-line justification each]
>
