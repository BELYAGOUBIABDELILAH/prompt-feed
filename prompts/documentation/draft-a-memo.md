# Draft a memo

**Category:** Documentation  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> Draft a clear, professional memo on the following topic:
> 
> To: [RECIPIENTS — team / department / leadership]
> From: [YOUR NAME / ROLE]
> Subject: [TOPIC]
> Context: [WHY THIS MEMO IS NEEDED]
> 
> ## Memo Structure
> 
> **DATE:** [Today's date]
> **TO:** [Recipients]
> **FROM:** [Sender]
> **SUBJECT:** [Clear, specific subject line]
> 
> ---
> 
> ### Background
> [2–3 sentences: why are you writing this now? What prompted it?]
> 
> ### Objective
> State clearly what this memo is trying to accomplish:
> - Inform and no action required?
> - Requesting a decision?
> - Proposing a change?
> - Documenting an agreement?
> 
> ### Key Points
> [3–5 bullet points — the essential information, each self-contained]
> 
> ### Success Criteria
> How will we know this objective was achieved?
> - [Measurable criterion 1]
> - [Measurable criterion 2]
> 
> ### Required Actions
> | Action | Owner | Deadline |
> |---|---|---|
> | [Action] | [Name] | [Date] |
> 
> ### Questions / Feedback
> [How recipients should respond — reply by email / attend meeting / no response needed]
> 
> ---
> Keep the memo to one page. Every sentence should earn its place.
