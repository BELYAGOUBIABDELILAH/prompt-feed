# Create Troubleshooting Guide

**Category:** Business & Career  
**Source:** GitLab Duo

## Prompt

> Create a troubleshooting guide for:
> 
> Issue area: [DESCRIBE — e.g., login failures, sync errors, payment issues]
> Audience: [customers / support agents / developers]
> Support tier: [L1 self-service / L2 agent / L3 engineering]
> 
> ## Troubleshooting Guide: [ISSUE AREA]
> 
> ### When to Use This Guide
> Use this guide when: [symptoms that indicate this issue]
> Do NOT use this guide if: [symptoms that indicate a different issue]
> 
> ### Quick Diagnosis (2 minutes)
> Answer these questions first:
> 1. [Question that quickly narrows down the cause]
> 2. [Question 2]
> → If [answer]: go to Section A
> → If [answer]: go to Section B
> 
> ---
> 
> ### Section A: [Common Cause 1]
> **Symptoms**: [what the user/system reports]
> 
> **Diagnostic steps**:
> 1. Check [WHAT]: [how to check it]
> 2. Look for [WHAT] in [WHERE]
> 
> **Fix**:
> [Step-by-step resolution]
> 
> **Verify**: After applying the fix, [how to confirm it worked]
> 
> ---
> 
> ### Section B: [Common Cause 2]
> [Same format]
> 
> ---
> 
> ### Escalation Criteria
> Escalate to [L2/L3/Engineering] if:
> - [ ] [Condition 1]
> - [ ] [Condition 2]
> - [ ] Issue persists after following all sections
> 
> **When escalating, include**:
> - [Info 1]
> - [Info 2]
> - [Logs or screenshots needed]
