# Research Documents for a Blog Post

**Category:** Research & Analysis  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Identify and summarize 3 key resources for a blog post on a specified topic.
> For each resource:
> 1. Title and source URL
> 2. Core argument or finding (2–3 sentences)
> 3. Key data point or quote worth citing
> 4. Why this source is credible and relevant
> 5. How it connects to the blog post angle
> Output: A research brief I can hand to a writer or use directly to draft the post.
