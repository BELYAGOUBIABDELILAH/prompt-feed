# Research Problems Faced by a User Persona

**Category:** Research & Analysis  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Identify and analyze top problems faced by a user persona in a specific activity.
> Persona: [DEFINE — role, company size, industry, goals]
> Activity: [DEFINE — what they're trying to accomplish]
> Output:
> 1. Top 5 problems ranked by severity and frequency
> 2. Current workarounds they use (and why those fall short)
> 3. Emotional frustrations associated with each problem
> 4. Jobs-to-be-done framing for each problem
> 5. Opportunity areas for a solution to capture
