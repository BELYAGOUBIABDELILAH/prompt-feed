# Customer Feedback Analysis

**Category:** Research & Analysis  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Analyze customer feedback over the past six months, highlighting key themes, positive feedback, and areas for improvement.
> Structure:
> 1. Top 5 themes by frequency (positive and negative)
> 2. Representative quotes for each theme
> 3. Severity ranking: which themes most impact retention or satisfaction
> 4. Recommended actions per theme
> 5. Metrics: NPS impact, ticket volume, feature request count
> Output: An executive-ready summary + a detailed breakdown by theme.
