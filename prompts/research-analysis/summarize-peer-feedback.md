# Summarize Peer Feedback

**Category:** Research & Analysis  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Summarize peer feedback for a performance review and align it with job expectations.
> Structure:
> 1. Themes: What are 3–5 recurring observations across reviewers?
> 2. Strengths: Specific behaviors mentioned positively (with frequency)
> 3. Development areas: Specific behaviors to improve (with context)
> 4. Alignment check: Map feedback to role expectations and competencies
> 5. Manager narrative: 2–3 paragraph summary written in the manager's voice, ready to use in the review
> Be objective. Preserve the signal. Remove the noise.
