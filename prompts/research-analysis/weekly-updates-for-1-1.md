# Weekly Updates for 1:1

**Category:** Research & Analysis  
**Source:** Glean

## Prompt

> Draft an update for your manager and an agenda for your weekly 1:1s.
> Update format (for async reading):
> - What I completed this week: [bullet list]
> - What I'm working on next week: [bullet list]
> - Blockers I need help with: [bullet list]
> - Metrics/progress: [key number or status]
> 1:1 agenda (for the meeting):
> 1. Quick wins to share (2 min)
> 2. Blocker I need a decision on (5 min)
> 3. Feedback I want (5 min)
> 4. Career/growth topic (optional, 5 min)
> Keep both under one page total.
